      Overview

      Namespaces represents the data of an untagged NAMESPACE response, returned by #namespace.

      Contains lists of #personal, #shared, and #other namespaces.

      Required capability

      Requires either NAMESPACE [RFC2342] or IMAP4rev2 capability.
