    #status  ⇒ String  (rw)
  
      

      
  
      
    
      The status of the event notification subscription.
      

      Constraints:
      

      Can be one of the following: creating | modifying | deleting |
active | no-permission | topic-not-exist
      

      The status "no-permission" indicates that ::Aws::Neptune no longer has
permission to post to the ::Aws::SNS topic. The status "topic-not-exist"
indicates that the topic was deleted after the subscription was
created.
